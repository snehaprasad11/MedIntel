# KPI Framework

## Average Waiting Time

### Formula

Total Waiting Time / Total Patients

### Business Importance

Measures patient experience and operational efficiency.

### Visualization

Line Chart

---

## Doctor Utilization

### Formula

Patient Service Time / Available Time

### Business Importance

Measures workload distribution.

### Visualization

Bar Chart

---

## Bed Occupancy Rate

### Formula

Occupied Beds / Total Beds

### Business Importance

Measures capacity utilization.

### Visualization

Gauge Chart

---

## Department Load

### Formula

Department Visits / Total Visits

### Business Importance

Identifies overloaded departments.

### Visualization

Heatmap

---

## Patient Throughput

### Formula

Patients Served Per Day

### Business Importance

Measures hospital productivity.

### Visualization

Trend Chart

---

## Lab Turnaround Time

### Formula

Result Time - Request Time

### Business Importance

Measures diagnostic efficiency.

### Visualization

Box Plot
